This is the recipe for making platemix. this is a dish with which you can go in nearly every direction to you heart's liking. i made this dish because i found myself consistenly having scrap pieces of vegetables. there is not much to tell about this dish, so let's just get to it!

what you need for to make platemix

  • potato slices or cubes
  • fish fillet steak
  • pieces of paprika
  • pieces of cucumber
  • couple of pickles
  • feta cheese
  • butter
  • spatula or 2
  • frying pan, 2 is recommended
  • cutting board and appropriate cutting knives

Preparation

  1. cut the fish in the desired cubes/pieces
  2. cut the paprika and cucumber in pieces of about 1cm
  3. cut the pickles in slices and break up the feta into crumbles, but keep them separate from the paprika and cucumber.

now that everything is cut and prepared it's time to heat up the frying pans

  1. put the butter into the frying pans
  2. put the potato cubes or slices in 1 frying pan and bake them well
  3. put the fish in the other frying pan and bake it, but at a slightly lower fire.
  4. when the potato's are nearly done put the paprika and cucumber in the same pan as the potato's and let them heat for a moment (1-2 min)
  5. when both the fish and the potato's are done, put them all together in a large bowl.
  6. put the pickle pieces in with it, and sprinkle the bowl with feta crumbs
  7. now it is time to dine like the gods themselves!

you can add sauce and/or spices to this dish to your liking. i prefer ketchup and a spice mix called "melange de France".
