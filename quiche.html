Heimdall's Quiche

This is the recipe for making quiche. Quiche is a French tart, with the basis of dough milk and eggs, with additions of meat, vegetables or anything. I sometimes feel i sound like a broken record but again, with this dish you can go any direction of your heart's desire.

what you need for to make quiche

  • about 5 slices of dough
  • 2 leek
  • about 250g bacon
  • 3 eggs
  • 250ml fresh creme
  • 75g rasped cheese
  • pepper
  • low cake pan
  • frying pan
  • oven
  • a medium to large sized bowl

let's get to work!

  1. preheat the oven on 200C and let de dough defrost.
  2. fat the low cake pan with a piece of butter and lay the dough in.
  3. push the overlapping parts of the dough good into each other so that there are no gaps.
  4. wash the leek and cut them into small rings.
  5. bake the bacon in the frying pan for 4-5 min, afterwards, lets them leak on a paper towel.
  6. bake the leek rings in the remain fat of the bacon for 4-5 min on a high fire and keep them moving/turning.
  7. break the eggs in the bowl with the creme and stir it loose.
  8. mix in the cheese, leek rings, bacon and some pepper.
  9. pour the mixture into the low cake pan
  10. put it into the oven and let it bake for 25-30 min.
  11. the quiche is done when you gently tap it with a fork, but the fork doesn't sink in.
  12. let the quiche rest for about 5 min when you take it out of the oven.

Heimdall says: Bon appetite!
